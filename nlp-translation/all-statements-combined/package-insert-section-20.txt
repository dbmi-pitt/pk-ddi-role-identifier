Sertraline hydrochloride is supplied for oral administration as scored tablets containing sertraline hydrochloride equivalent to 25, 50, 100, 150 and 200 mg of sertraline and the following inactive ingredients: dibasic calcium phosphate dihydrate, hydroxypropyl cellulose, hypromellose, magnesium stearate, microcrystalline cellulose, polyethylene glycol, sodium starch glycolate, talc, and titanium dioxide.
